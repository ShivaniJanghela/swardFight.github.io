About

FightingGame a multiplayer game, where players fight each other with diffrent 2D swords,and try to decrese each others life bar. player whose lifebar empty first dies and the other one wines...try to empty other player lifebar first.

Key Controls

player1

A - move left
D - move right
W - jump
Q - attack

player2

Arrowleft - move left
Arrowlright - move right
Arrowup - jump
Space- attack
